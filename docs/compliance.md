# Compliance Boundary

This repository implements a virtual strategy-game prototype. It must not be treated as a real-money gambling product.

## In Scope

- Virtual chips, fictional votes, score, rank, and replay/audit artifacts.
- Original names, UI, code, and mechanics inspired by abstract game-design patterns.
- Deterministic fairness verification and optional ZK proof research.

## Out of Scope Without Separate Review

- Real-money wagers.
- Redeemable tokens, prizes, sweepstakes, or cash-equivalent rewards.
- Custodial wallets, deposits, withdrawals, or exchange integrations.
- Minors, KYC/AML, tax reporting, and regulated betting jurisdictions.

Any move toward real-money or redeemable value requires legal review, licensing analysis, age gates, KYC/AML, responsible-play controls, consumer-protection review, and jurisdiction-specific tax handling.
